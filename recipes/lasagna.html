Lasagna!

Here is the simplest and most vague lasagna recipe you will find.

I'm only putting the text in to determine if I can do this right Maybe someday I'll create my own recipe site.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Flat noodles

Instructions

  1. turn oven to 375 degrees
  2. Cook the noodles until they're al dente
  3. Spread a layer of sauce on bottom of 9x13 pan
  4. add a layer of overlapping noodles
  5. add layer of cheese
  6. add sauce on top of cheese
  7. add layer of noodles
  8. add a layer of cheese
  9. add sauce
  10. add noodles
  11. add sauce
  12. add cheese
  13. Put lasagna in oven for 1 hour
  14. Remove and eat
