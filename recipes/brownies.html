Killer Brownies!

Face it, we're all tired of this pandemic. There's really only one thing that makes sense now. That's eating more!

This brownie recipe is dead simple and may, in fact, kill you.

Good luck, you'll need it

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 bag chocolate chips
  • 2 sticks of butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 large chicken eggs
  • 3 Tbsp water
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees (Fahrenheit)
  2. It's real simple - "mise en place". Look it up. It means assemble all your ingredients and tools before cooking. Seriously, don't be a noob.
  3. Bung that sugar, water and butter into a 2 quart saucepan (or whatever you have)
  4. Heat the sugar, butter, and water over medium heat; stirring constantly, until all the butter and sugar melts
  5. Once the sugar is just starting to boil, remove from heat
  6. Add vanilla to molten lava sugar
  7. Mix half a bag of chocolate chips into molten lava sugar. Stir until it looks like chocolate sludge.
  8. Bung the chocolate sauce into mixing bowl, preferably you have a mixer
  9. Start mixing the chocolate sludge in that there mixing bowl on medium speed. If you don't have a mixer, use a whisk and beat it like a rented mule.
  10. Add the eggs one at a time. Don't be a knob and forget to crack the eggs. No one wants eggshells in their brownies.
  11. Add the flour, salt, baking powder while mixing
  12. Add the rest of the chocolate chips
  13. Bung the batter into a greased and floured 8x8 pan
  14. Cook for 35 minutes
  15. Cool them before you eat them, ya git
